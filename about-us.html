Culture & Background

Innovation is at the core of Qadir Corporation. We foster a culture of curiosity, experimentation…

Established in 2008, Qadir Corporation is at the forefront of advancing robotic automation through cutting-edge innovation.

Our mission is to develop versatile and intelligent robotic systems that seamlessly integrate into various industries. By leveraging AI, machine learning, and robotics, we streamline processes, enhance productivity, and drive efficiency for our clients.

Our goal is to make robotic automation an integral part of industries, transforming the way businesses operate.

Today, the company stands proud as Taiwan’s leading robotic automation design house in the Taiwan and East Europe.
